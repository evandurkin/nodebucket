nodebucket was created with the idea
that keeping track of tasks should be a
simple, straightforward, and visual process.

Keep track of your tasks by writing them out
and then drag-and-drop them to the side when
you've completed them.

Staying on track is as simple as that.



How to manage your tasks

Step 1 On the Home page click the Create a Task button to add a new task
Step 2 Reorder your tasks by clicking + dragging them within the column
Step 3 To delete a task, click the trash can icon and confirm
Step 4 When your task is finished, drag-and-drop the task over to the Done column
